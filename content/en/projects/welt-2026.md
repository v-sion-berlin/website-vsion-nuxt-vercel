Welt

#project-name
Refresh 2026

#body
The news channel [Welt](https://www.welt.de/tv-programm-live-stream/){:target="_blank"} launched its brand refresh in March 2026. The visual identity was carefully modernized without changing the recognizability of the brand.

#sub
We handled the technical implementation of the new design into the real-time systems — including programming the logics, developing the templates, and integrating the new on-air elements. We also played a key role in the automation of the mixerless control room.
