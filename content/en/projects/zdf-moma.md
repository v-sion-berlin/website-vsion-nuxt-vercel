#project-name
moma

#body
This is what the new [ZDF Morgenmagazin](https://www.zdf.de/magazine/zdf-morgenmagazin-104){:target="_blank"} "moma" looks like! The redesign of the studio is more than just a makeover for ZDF: the television studio becomes a space where viewers can experience the work of the journalism teams.

#sub
The design was developed by our partner agency [CapeRock](https://www.caperock.tv){:target="_blank"} (many thanks for the video!) from Amsterdam. Once again, we worked together with ZDF in a wonderfully collaborative way.
