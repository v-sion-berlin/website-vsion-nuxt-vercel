Welt

#project-name
US Election 2024

#body
When millions are watching, everything must work. And it did: over ten hours live — with real-time data, interactive and immersive.

#sub
For the live coverage, we took the election broadcast to a new level:

Interactive election data down to county level — directly controllable by touch; Automated graphics on Vidiwalls in real time; Dynamic studio lighting in red or blue depending on the election result; Seamless social media integration; Augmented graphics for the "Presidential Race".

We receive the results data from AP and connect it to all systems.
