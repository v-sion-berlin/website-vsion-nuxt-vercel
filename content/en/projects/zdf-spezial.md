#project-name
Spezial

#body
The shows are spontaneously produced and broadcast [live](https://www.zdf.de/magazine/zdfspezial-128) for special events. The extraordinary events are put into context by ZDF's journalistic network.

#sub
We implement the graphics and build the opener for the real-time system, enabling rapid production. Many thanks to [BDA Creative](https://www.bdacreative.com/) from Munich for the design and the video!
