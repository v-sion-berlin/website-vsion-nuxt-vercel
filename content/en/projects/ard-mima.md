#project-name
MiMa

#body
The [ARD Mittagsmagazin](https://www.ardmediathek.de/sendung/ard-mittagsmagazin/Y3JpZDovL2Rhc2Vyc3RlLmRlL2FyZC1taXR0YWdzbWFnYXppbg){:target="_blank"} has moved from Munich to Berlin after 28 years and now broadcasts from the ZDF television studios Unter den Linden.

#sub
We developed a playback and broadcast concept for the 3 Vidiwalls in the television studio, including preview functionalities and easy operation for the news team. Everything was implemented in just 3 weeks, and we are delighted about a successful broadcast launch from the heart of Berlin.
