#project-name
Terra X

#body
For the web media team at ZDF, we produce short [social media clips](https://www.facebook.com/ZDFterraX/?locale=de_DE){:target="_blank"} on curious topics: informative, surprising, and with a wink. Idea, storytelling, illustration, and animation are entirely handled by us, in the style of Terra X.

#sub
Together with the editorial team, we develop topics, tell incisive stories, and bring them to life visually. From the initial idea through illustration and design to animation, we accompany the entire process.

The result is short clips with high recognition value that convey knowledge in an entertaining way and are optimized for social media. The following examples show a selection of our completed contributions.
